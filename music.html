Juice WRLD Discography

Studio Albums

Goodbye & Good Riddance (2018)

Juice WRLD's debut album that brought him mainstream success

Top Tracks: "Lucid Dreams", "All Girls Are the Same", "Lean Wit Me"

Death Race for Love (2019)

His second studio album that debuted at number one on the Billboard 200

Top Tracks: "Robbery", "Hear Me Calling", "Fast"

Legends Never Die (2020)

Posthumous album released after his tragic passing

Top Tracks: "Come & Go", "Wishing Well", "Life's a Mess"

Fighting Demons (2021)

Second posthumous album with powerful emotional themes

Top Tracks: "Already Dead", "Burn", "Girl of My Dreams"

The Party Never Ends (2024)

Third posthumous album continuing his legacy

Top Tracks: "Swerve", "To the Stars", "Doomsday"

Musical Journey

2015

Began posting music on SoundCloud under the name JuicetheKidd

2017

Released EP "9 9 9" and changed his name to Juice WRLD

2018

Released "Lucid Dreams" and signed to Interscope Records for $3 million

Released debut album "Goodbye & Good Riddance"

2019

Released "Death Race for Love" which debuted at #1 on Billboard 200

Collaborated with artists including BTS, Ellie Goulding, and YoungBoy Never Broke Again

2020-2024

Posthumous releases including "Legends Never Die", "Fighting Demons", and "The Party Never Ends"

Continued to break streaming records and maintain a musical legacy
